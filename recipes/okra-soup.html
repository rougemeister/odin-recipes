Okro Soup

A Delicious Bowl of Green Chicken and Beef Okro Soup

Okro Soup, is a very popular and delicious stew that is loved by the majority of the people, in West Africa, especially if cooked well.

Okra soup is a fascinating dish because of its many permutations across West Africa and its reinvention in the Americas as gumbo.

Ingredients

  • One pound fresh okra
  • ½ pound meat of beef/li>
  • ½ pound Shrimp
  • ½ cup medium sized Onions chopped
  • ½ cup ground Crayfish
  • 1 tablespoon Beef or chicken bouillon
  • 3 cups of chopped Spinach
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon red pepper flakes
  • ½ cup ground Egusi optional
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start by creating a base stock for the okro soup by boiling the roast beef or any other meat of choice with salt, seasoning cube and onions.
  2. Cook till tender.
  3. While the meat is boiling, mince half of the onions in a food processor or a chopper and slice the other half.
  4. Mince the red bell pepper and habanero pepper in the chopper and set aside
  5. Once the meat is tender, add the minced pepper, onions, crayfish, and locust bean and leave to cook for about 10 minutes.
  6. Shred and add the washed dry fish and leave to soften for about 5 minutes.
  7. Add the palm oil and the shrimps and the minced and sliced okro.
  8. Leave to cook for about 3 to 5 minutes depending on how crunchy you want the okro to be.
  9. Stir in the spinach and mix until wilted.
  10. Take away from heat immediately and serve.
